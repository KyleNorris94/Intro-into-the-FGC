The 'Comeback kid' history that defined fighting games

The moment happened while at EVO championship series 2004 between Daigo U. and Justin W.

During this match, Umehara made an unexpected comeback by parrying 15 consecutive hits of Wong's "Super Art" move while having only one pixel of vitality.
Another reason why it has influenced the fighting game community is because it had happened during the fighting community's "dark ages"
in which most of the scene's fans had been the same groups that group up with the original fighters of the late 80s-90s era.

In fact, its legacy is so renown, it has been compared to such feats as Babe Ruth's called run or the famed hockey 'Miracle on Ice'
Since then it has been repeated a number of times, once in a charity event with again Daigo and Justin, and another in a friendly match.

Kyle Norris. Olympic College, Bremerton WA
